About Automotive Engines

Automotive engines are the powerhouse of vehicles, transforming fuel into motion and making transportation possible. They come in various types, including internal combustion, electric, and hybrid engines.

Diesel Engines

Diesel engines are known for their efficiency and durability. They operate using compression ignition, where air is compressed to a high temperature, and fuel is injected to ignite. Diesel engines are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and buses due to their higher torque output and fuel economy.

Petrol Engines

Petrol engines, or gasoline engines, use spark ignition to combust a mixture of air and fuel. These engines are popular in most passenger vehicles due to their smoother operation and lower emissions compared to diesel engines. Petrol engines are also lighter and offer better acceleration.
